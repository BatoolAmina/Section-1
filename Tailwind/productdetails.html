Product Description

Elevate your living space with this exquisite 3-seater sofa that comes with two ottomans, meticulously crafted to blend luxury with functionality. This stunning piece boasts a harmonious combination of half-leather upholstery, featuring sumptuous bovine leather on body-touching parts and durable faux leather elsewhere. Experience the indulgent warmth and breathability of natural leather, coupled with the practicality of faux leather for easy maintenance and longevity. Sink into unparalleled comfort with these thoughtfully designed seat cushions, featuring high-quality foam and wadding for optimal support. Supported by a sturdy pinewood frame, pretreated against moisture and wood infestation, this sofa is as durable as it is elegant. The webbing and zig-zag springs at the base ensure stability and resilience, providing a solid foundation for the seat cushions. Enjoy the extra seat depth and sink into the plush back cushions, filled with a luxurious blend of polyfill and feathers for an extra layer of comfort.
